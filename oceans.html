Cleaning our Oceans

Using our drones we can pinpoint locations of high trash. Additionally, with inputs from the community on our blog site, we can more accurately send these drones to vacuum and pick up garbage.

Benefits of cleaning our oceans

  1. The oceans contain 97% of the earth's water
  2. Living Oceans absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen
  3. Marine life provides at least one-sixth of the protein people consume
  4. If we do not clean our oceans, beaches will become unusable

An example of pollution in oceans today

How will we use the ocean to reduce global warming?

Through some processes we can extract CO2 from the ocean's waters. This CO2 can be used to grow plants or used as fuel for vehicles.
